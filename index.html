About Tanisha ❤️

I don’t think there’s a way to fully explain Tanisha — but I’ll try.

She’s funny in the most effortless way, says the most unexpected things, and has this energy that’s somewhere between chaotic and childlike and completely genuine.

There’s something about her that makes ordinary things feel a little softer — like playing cards over a video call, or solving a Wordle together at midnight, that somehow turns into a ritual.
